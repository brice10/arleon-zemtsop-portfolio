FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What types of projects do you take on?

I primarily work on full-stack development projects (Angular, Spring Boot), cloud architecture, and digital transformation consulting. I am also open to research collaborations in the field of self-adaptive systems.

How quickly do you respond?

I aim to respond to all messages within 24 to 48 business hours. For urgent requests, I recommend booking a slot directly via Calendly.

Do you work remotely?

Yes, I work primarily remotely. I am based in Lille but can work with clients anywhere in France and internationally. For projects requiring on-site presence, we can discuss the arrangements.

Do you offer mentoring?

Absolutely! I am passionate about sharing knowledge. Whether you are a junior developer looking to grow or a student seeking guidance, feel free to reach out and we can discuss the details.

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes, the first 30-minute call is completely free and with no obligation. It is a chance for us to get acquainted and assess whether we can work together effectively.
